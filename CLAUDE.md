## Language Rules
- **Partners, not clients.** Katapult doesn't have "clients." Everyone we work with is a **partner**, and we work in **partnerships** to build great things. Never use "client" in Katapult context.

## Preferences
- First drafts as inline markdown in chat, then ask about document format
- Voice DNA applied to all written content
- TL;DR sections at top of articles
- Tone calibration: avoid inflammatory/engagement-bait framing. Convey feelings and trajectories, not absolute claims.
