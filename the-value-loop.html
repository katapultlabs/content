The General Principle
Every domain has its own definition of valuable. Every output has its own fidelity requirements.

Harness engineering is one value loop, optimized for one domain. We're looking for the general principle underneath.

What This Means

The scarce skill

Raw output is increasingly abundant. The judgment about how to structure collaboration? That's the scarce skill.

The lawyer
who structures the right value loop for litigation
The designer
who knows when to let AI generate 50 variants and when to insist on hand-crafted specificity
The engineer
who builds the scaffolding that turns raw intelligence into reliable systems

The value loop is about humans owning the thing only humans can own: the definition of value, and the accountability for whether the system delivered it.
